Conduit Foundation

Make all new construction ready for electric cars.

200 million charging spots

The United States has two billion parking spots. We will have 200 million new parking spots in ten years, assuming 1% new construction every year. The new parking spots can be electric vehicle ready.

This can be accomplished by making a simple change to building code: require conduit to be laid out and twenty percent of parking lot wired and ready. Conduit future-proofs the lot, can make it 100% EV anytime.
